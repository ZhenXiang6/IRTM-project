The US House on Wednesdayapproved legislation that could ban TikTokin the United States over concerns about the video sharing platform’s Chinese ownership.
Thebill, which passed on a bipartisan 352-65 vote, would require the app to part ways with its Chinese parent company, ByteDance, within 180 daysor face a ban in American app stores. The measure is now expected to move to the US Senate, where it faces an uncertain future. President Joe Biden has said hewould be prepared to signthe bill if it passes both chambers.
