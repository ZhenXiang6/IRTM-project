The COMPUTEX forum in Taipei welcomed top tech heavyweights, including Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang, whose company has just passed Apple as the world's second-most valuable company. In his televised keynote speech ahead of COMPUTEX, he called Taiwan "a pillar of the world" as a reference to the island's integral position in the artificial intelligence revolution. CNN's Will Ripley has more.
